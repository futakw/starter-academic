I study **Trustworthy AI**, focusing on adversarial robustness and reliable generalization from **mechanism to deployment**:
- I analyze why models fail under adversarial perturbation (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.14337" style="text-decoration: underline;">WACV'23</a>).
- I design robust training and defense methods for computer vision (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.15519" style="text-decoration: underline;">ICIP'24</a>, <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.14648" style="text-decoration: underline;">ICLR'25</a>).
- I extend these ideas to vision-language systems (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.18770" style="text-decoration: underline;">WACV'26</a>, <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.16257" style="text-decoration: underline;">ACMMM'25</a>).
- I also work on complementary reliability and security topics, including post-hoc calibration (<a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2302.05118" style="text-decoration: underline;">ICML'23</a>) and model IP protection (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.08604v4" style="text-decoration: underline;">ACL'25 Main</a>).

I have collaborated widely across academia and industry through joint research and internships, including work with <a href="https://www.tum.de/en/" style="text-decoration: underline;">TUM</a>, <a href="https://www.nii.ac.jp/en/" style="text-decoration: underline;">NII</a>, <a href="https://www.nec.com/" style="text-decoration: underline;">NEC</a>, <a href="https://cyberagent.ai/ailab/" style="text-decoration: underline;">CyberAgent AI Lab</a>, <a href="https://www.sbintuitions.co.jp/" style="text-decoration: underline;">SB Intuitions</a>, and <a href="https://tur.ing/en" style="text-decoration: underline;">Turing</a>.
